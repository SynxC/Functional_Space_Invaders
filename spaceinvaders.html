Space Invader Gameplay Instructions

Welcome to Space invaders! Please Refer to the controls on the keys required to interact with the game below.

Gameplay

1. Kill all the invaders.

2. Avoid Bullets of invaders.

3. Get hit and you're dead.

4. Shields do not replenish. So only sacrifice if needed.

5. Have fun!
